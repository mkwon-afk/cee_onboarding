CEE at a Glance

Who makes up the Customer Experience Engineering org?

The Customer Experience Engineering (CEE) organization is Red Hat's customer-facing support umbrella, spanning five specialized teams.

Technical Support (GSS)
Global Support Services — technical troubleshooting, product issues, case management
Customer Service
Account management, subscriptions, user access, order inquiries — 24/5, multilingual
Managed Cloud Services (MCS)
Cloud-native support for ARO, ROSA, and FedRAMP-regulated environments
Secure Global Support (SGS)
Specialized secure support for regulated industries and government customers
Solutions Support Delivery
Complex solution architecture and delivery support for enterprise customers
Use CEE NotebookLM as your first stop to find team contacts, reach the right group, and navigate supportability policies.

Proactive Support Engagement

Scheduled maintenance window? Open a Severity 3 case two weeks early. Begin the summary with "Proactive:" and include activities, start/end date and time, duration, diagnostic files (SOSreport), and primary contact information. If issues arise during the window, call the support hotline, provide the case number, and raise severity as needed.
Always check the Supportability Matrix before engaging GSS to confirm the issue falls within supported scope.

Cloud Services (MCS)

Managed Cloud & FedRAMP

The Managed Cloud Services (MCS) team specializes in supporting Red Hat's cloud-native offerings, with a focus on FedRAMP-regulated environments and joint support workflows with cloud partners.
